Grooming

Professional grooming from express baths to full breed-specific styling. Your dog leaves looking and smelling absolutely amazing.

Daycare

Cage-free, supervised play all day in our spacious indoor & outdoor yards. Full-day and half-day options to fit your schedule.

  • Cage-free open play environment
  • Temperament-sorted playgroups
  • Structured play & nap rotations
  • Photo updates throughout the day

  • 24/7 on-site overnight supervision
  • Daily photo report cards sent to you
  • Complimentary exit bath available
Starting at $45/night  ·  discounts for extended stays
