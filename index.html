06 —Questions, Answered

The fine print,
in plain English.

Everything you've wondered about — and a few things you might not have thought to ask.

A full groom covers a complete haircut, bath and blow-dry, brush out, nail trim and grind, teeth cleaning, and anal gland expression. Finished with a bow or bandana. Bath & brush packages exclude trimming and gland expression.
Yes. All dogs must be current on Rabies, DHPP (distemper combo), and Bordetella. We require documentation before the first stay or play day for the health of every dog in our care.
Grooming time is driven by coat density, length, and condition. A short-coat terrier takes very different time than a double-coated Samoyed or a curly doodle. We publish our exact rates by breed to keep things transparent — no surprise upsells at pickup.
Daycare is supervised play during open hours (half day or full day). Boarding includes overnight stay in a private suite with six outdoor breaks, two indoor playroom sessions, bedtime routine, and 24/7 on-site care. You can add grooming and spa services to either.
We're Fear-Free certified and specialize in this. We do short meet-and-greets before full appointments, work at your dog's pace, and never restrain unnecessarily. Special handling fees apply only when truly warranted, and we explain them clearly.
Call or text (407) 833-3880, email fancypetsfl@gmail.com, or stop by 870 S Sun Drive, Suite 1054. We'll match you with the right service and confirm pricing based on your dog's breed and condition.
